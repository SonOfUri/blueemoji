Tokenomics Section

Our token, BLUEEMOJI (BLU), is a deflationary cryptocurrency built on the Ethereum blockchain. With a total supply of 1,000,000 BLU, our tokenomics are designed to reward holders and promote scarcity. Every transaction incurs a 2% fee, with 1% being burned and 1% redistributed to existing holders. This ensures a decreasing supply over time, increasing the value of each BLU in circulation. Get ready to ride the wave of blue and watch your holdings grow!
