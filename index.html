What are EtherEmojis?

EtherEmojis are a collection of programmatically, randomly generated NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain. The originals consist of 10,000 randomly assembled emojis and backgrounds. Each EtherEmoji is unique and different!

Why get an EtherEmoji?

It's your chance to own a piece of emoji culture. In addition, an investment in EtherEmojis is an investment in mental health. Emojis are the expression of emotions and we all know that expressing your emotions is crucial for mental well being. We're giving 5% of ETH raised to a mental health organization chosen by the community.

We’re also giving back 10% of all ETH raised to the community (through contests, raffles, and more) - because we genuinely want EtherEmojis to be a community driven and centric project.

When you get an EtherEmoji, you get a voice in the community and the power to guide the direction of the project. We need help from you and other EtherEmoji hodlers to make decisions on projects like merch, emoji morphs, Gen2, EmojiWorld and more!
